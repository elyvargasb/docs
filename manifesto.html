We are based in Catford, South East London. We are passionate about creating blended products that are ethically sourced, organic, taste good & as a bonus have great healthy benefits.

We are not a cleansing or detox drinks company. We just see our juices and smoothies as a compliment to your daily eating and drinking habits.

We are curious & excited by indigenous fruits & veg not commonly known in our part of town or used widely, but possess incredible nutritional values and taste fab when blended together. (We do not use HPP processes, additives or preservatives).
